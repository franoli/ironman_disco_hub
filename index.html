IRONMAN Mobile App

Official mobile companion for athletes worldwide

Executive Summary

We are launching the official IRONMAN Mobile App. This app will serve as a personalized companion for athletes, offering seamless registration, centralized results, live race tracking, and post-race insights, while also supporting spectators and operational stakeholders.

The mobile app will consolidate critical athlete services previously scattered across multiple platforms. The goal is to reduce friction across the athlete's journey, from registration through race day and beyond, while introducing new opportunities for loyalty, cross-sell, and engagement.

This app will integrate with existing systems, external timing and registration partners, and customer support & commerce tools to deliver a scalable foundation for innovation, personalization, and operational efficiency.

Vision & Goals

Strategic direction and success criteria from PRD

üìÑ View PRD Source Document ‚Üí

‚úì Goals

Best-in-Class Mobile App

Create a mobile app for IRONMAN athletes that centralizes event discovery, registration, profile management, race tracking, and post-race engagement.

Personalized Experience

Deliver a personalized athlete experience by leveraging athlete history, milestones, and performance data to tailor content and recommendations.

Support Entire Race Journey

From onboarding and account creation to race day execution and post-event highlights, enabling athletes to plan, perform, and reflect seamlessly.

Live Tracking for Supporters

Enable live tracking and race visibility for friends, family, and spectators during events to extend the IRONMAN experience beyond the athlete.

Scalable Multi-Brand Platform

Build a platform that can support multiple IRONMAN brands (RnR, Epic, etc.) and evolve with future business needs.

Drive Engagement & Cross-sell

Surface relevant events, merchandise, and content based on athlete preferences and behaviors.

Reduce Support Tickets

Improve self-service via a centralized hub for waivers, deferrals, transfers, and other race services.

‚úó Non-Goals (Out of Scope)

‚úó

Not a Platform Replacement

The app is not intended to replace core registration platforms or timing systems; it will integrate with them.

‚úó

Not Fan-Only

This is not a fan-only app. The primary user is the athlete, though we will support spectator experiences via features like athlete tracking.

‚úó

Not for Event Staff/Volunteers

This release is not focused on event staff/volunteer tooling (though future phases may explore operational modules).

‚úó

No Full E-commerce in V1

The app will not offer full merchandising or e-commerce functionality in v1; product linking will redirect to existing stores.
